My Story

I founded Surf or Sleep due to the frustrating inconsistencies in surf conditions reported by various websites. Learning to surf, I encountered conflicting data that didn't match the actual conditions when I hit the waves. Month after month, the struggle persisted. Determined to create a solution, Surf or Sleep was born. It's a platform that emerged from the need for reliable and consistent surf data, ensuring a better surfing experience for enthusiasts like me.

My Objective

When creating Surf or Sleep, I had three key objectives. First, I aimed to aggregate surf conditions from various sites, creating a consensus by averaging them out. Second, I wanted to provide a daily email to users at 6 am, empowering them to decide whether to surf or sleep based on the latest conditions. Lastly, I envisioned a personalized experience by considering each user's preferences and matching them with the daily surf conditions. This way, our daily emails offer tailored recommendations telling each use if they should Surf or Sleep.

My Goals

As the beta version of our website unfolds, we have ambitious goals set for its full development. By the end of 2024, our primary objectives include:

  • Expanded Surf Conditions: Extend surf condition coverage beyond Huntington Beach. Anticipate updates for Newport Beach, Doheney, and Trestles to provide a comprehensive overview of Orange County conditions.
  • User Account Features: Introduce a dedicated account tab, empowering users to personalize their site settings and adjust preferences as they progress in their surfing journey.
  • UI Enhancements: Implement ongoing UI enhancements to ensure a consistently improved user experience. Our commitment is to make regular updates, refining and optimizing the website interface for seamless navigation.
